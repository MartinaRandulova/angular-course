My recipe

Ingredients:

  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon ground ginger
  • 1 3/4 teaspoons ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground cloves
  • 6 tablespoons unsalted butter, softened
  • 3/4 cup dark brown sugar, packed
  • 1 large egg
  • 1/2 cup molasses
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Instructions

Preheat your oven: Preheat your oven to 350째F (175째C).

Prepare dry ingredients: In a medium bowl, whisk together the flour, baking powder, baking soda, salt, ginger, cinnamon, and cloves. Set aside.

Cream butter and sugar: In a large bowl, cream together the softened butter and brown sugar until light and fluffy.

Add wet ingredients: Beat in the egg, and then add the molasses and vanilla extract. Mix until well combined.

Combine wet and dry ingredients: Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients, mixing well after each addition until a dough forms.

Chill the dough: Divide the dough in half, shape each half into a disc, wrap them in plastic wrap, and refrigerate for at least 1 hour. Chilling the dough makes it easier to handle and helps the cookies keep their shape during baking.

Roll out the dough: Preheat your oven to 350째F (175째C) again. On a lightly floured surface, roll out one disc of dough to about 1/4 inch thickness.

Cut out shapes: Use cookie cutters to cut out your desired shapes and transfer them to a parchment paper-lined baking sheet, leaving some space between each cookie.

Bake: Bake in the preheated oven for 8-10 minutes, or until the edges are lightly browned. Keep a close eye on them, as baking times may vary depending on the size of your cookies.

Cool: Allow the cookies to cool on the baking sheet for a few minutes before transferring them to a wire rack to cool completely.

Decorate: Once the cookies are completely cool, you can decorate them with icing, sprinkles, or other toppings as desired.

Enjoy your homemade gingerbread cookies!
